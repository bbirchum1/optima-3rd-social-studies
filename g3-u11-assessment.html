Part B: Exhibition Response
Questions 7–9 · 18 Points (6 pts each)
Directions: For each question below, think back to everything you studied this year. Use specific names, places, and ideas from the course. Your answers should be 3–5 sentences each.
Exhibition Response 6 pts
7Map Connection
Name one place you studied this year. Describe it using geographic detail — where is it located? What landforms, bodies of water, or regions are nearby? Why does its location matter?
Exhibition Response 6 pts
8Source Connection
Name one source (a document, photograph, map, artifact, or other primary source) that you studied this year. What did you learn from it? Why does it matter to people today?
Exhibition Response 6 pts
9Economics or Civics Connection
Pick one economics concept (scarcity, trade, goods and services, opportunity cost) OR one civics concept (civic virtue, government, citizenship, rule of law). Define the concept and explain why it is important for communities.
