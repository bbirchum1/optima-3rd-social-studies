Part B: Council Chamber Scenario
Question 4 · 10 Points
🏛 Regional Council Scenario A family wants to move somewhere with warm weather, good farmland for growing fruits and vegetables, and a city near water for trade. They have asked the Regional Council for a recommendation. Your job is to recommend one U.S. region and explain why it fits.
4 Extended Written Response 10 pts
Which region do you recommend? Give at least two pieces of geographic evidence — climate, landforms, resources, or bodies of water — to support your choice. Explain how each piece of evidence matches what the family needs.
