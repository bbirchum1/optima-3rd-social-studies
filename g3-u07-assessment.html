Part B: Source Task
Questions 8–9 · 7 Points
📄 Primary Source — "Home on the Range" The song "Home on the Range" was written around the 1870s by a Kansas doctor. Cowboys sang it on cattle drives across the Great Plains. It describes wide open land, buffalo, and big skies. Kansas made it the official state song in 1947.
